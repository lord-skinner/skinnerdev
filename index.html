About Me

Hello, I'm Matthew. You can call me Skinner. I'm a father, husband, nerd, and an experienced developer.

I spent my childhood summers hacking on things in my grandfather's garage, soldering wires onto the hardware bus of microcontrollers and learning to compile and push my own code down to them. I formed a real passion for building and tech early on and have been fortunate enough to turn that passion into a somewhat successful career.

I spent my early career doing what is now called Full Stack Development. I built a small business around web design and development from 2002 to 2012. From there, I took a small break from tech. I went off to learn about business and management, contracts, procurement, and general business operations.

In 2018, I found my way back to tech. As a developer I thought of my craft as writing code and building applications and not as "data". However, I quickly realized that data is at the core of everything we do in tech. From applications to infrastructure, data is being created, moved, transformed, and stored at every layer. I dove head first into data engineering, data architecture, and cloud architecture. Since then, I have been fortunate enough to work with some amazing teams and companies building enterprise grade data solutions.
